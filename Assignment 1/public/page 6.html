What You'll Need

  • pale foundation/wite facepaint
  • blusher
  • white eyeline
  • black eyeline
  • fake eyelashes
  • red lipstick

How To

Apply your pale pace evenly all over your face, to help achieve that porcaline doll look

Apply generous amounts of blusher to your cheeks, along your cheek bones

Outline your yes with the white eyeline to enlarge them. Then outline your white outline with your black eyeliner

Stick your fake lashes to the outer side ouf the black eyeliner. This will make your eyes appear larger then they really are

Apply your red lipstick and any other details you can think of!
