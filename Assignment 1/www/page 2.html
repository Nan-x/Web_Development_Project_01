What You'll Need

  • foundation of your choice
  • fishnet tights or hairnet
  • bright coloured eyeshadow
  • highligher
  • white eyeline

How To

Apply your perfered foundation as your base all over your face

Pull your fishnet tights or hair net over your face, you'll look a sight!

Using your bright coloured eyeshadow and a sponge dab over the net to create scales. Try using a few overlapping colours!

Use your highlighter on your cheekbones and forehead over your eyeshadow to add a shine to your scales!

Use your white eyeliner to add some smaller details

Add extra glitter to this look really makes it pop!
